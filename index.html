    • The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel

      The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel is an American period comedy-drama television series created by Amy Sherman-Palladino that debuted on Amazon Prime Video on March 17, 2017. It stars Rachel Brosnahan as Miriam "Midge" Maisel, a New York housewife who discovers she has a talent for stand-up comedy and pursues a career in it. It is set in the late 1950s and early 1960s. Alex Borstein, Michael Zegen, Marin Hinkle, Tony Shalhoub, Kevin Pollak, Caroline Aaron, Jane Lynch, and Luke Kirby also appear. The series was picked up by Amazon Studios after the pilot episode received critical acclaim. Amazon renewed the series for a fifth and final season on February 17, 2022.
